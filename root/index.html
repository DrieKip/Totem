TOTEM

Controls

In Totem, the player plays as a totem pole that can split into multiple blocks. Use the arrow keys to move the totem pole, and press the spacebar to split it into multiple blocks.

Enemies

Watch out for enemies roaming the level. Some are stationary, while others will move around and try to block your path.

Art

The art style is vibrant and colorful, with the totem pole and enemies represented by various shades of purple, green, and blue.
