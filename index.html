Totem

Controls

In Totem, the player plays as a totem pole that can split into multiple blocks. The player can move the blocks around, jump, and combine them to form the totem pole again.

Enemies

The enemies in Totem are colourful and varied. They come in the form of snakes, spiders, bats, and other creatures. The player must use their blocks to defend themselves from the enemies and progress through the levels.

Art

The art in Totem is vibrant and full of life. The colours used are purple, green, and blue, and the art style is cartoon-like. The player will be able to explore a variety of levels, each with their own theme and challenges.
